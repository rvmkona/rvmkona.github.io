Independent research at the intersection of physics, biology, ecology, and sustainable civilization. Our Research Get in Touch Mission Understanding how systems find and lose coherence. The Pacific Coherence Institute conducts independent interdisciplinary research into the principles that govern coherent systems - from quantum fields to living cells to ecological watersheds to human communities. We develop open-source frameworks, tools, and standards that make these principles accessible across scientific disciplines, clinical practice, and community design. All research is published openly under Creative Commons licenses. Our work begins with a simple observation: the same organizational principles appear at every scale of nature - in the timing of a heartbeat, the structure of a watershed, the geometry of a galaxy. The highest-fidelity science is that which maintains the relationship between human activity and the natural world it emerged from. Understanding why this is so - and what it means for physics, medicine, ecology, and civilization - is the work of the Institute. Research Coherence Science One framework. Every scale. Grounded in the watershed. The Pacific Coherence Institute is based on the Big Island of Hawaii. The Kohala and Kona coasts - their reefs, watersheds, and agricultural lands - are not just our home. They are our laboratory and our first measure of whether the science is working. Aloha Aina - Love of the Land. We hold that the health of a place is measurable - in its soil, its water, its reef, its silence. The Institute is committed to developing the tools that make this measurement possible, and to ensuring that the communities who steward these places have access to them first. Connect Get involved.
